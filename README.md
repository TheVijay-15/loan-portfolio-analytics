# Fintech Loan Portfolio Analytics

A comprehensive data analytics project analyzing Lending Club loan data to optimize profitability and risk management using SQL, Python, and interactive Streamlit dashboard.

## Business Problem Statement

**Title:** Optimizing Loan Portfolio Profitability and Risk Management in a Digital Lending Platform

### Context
Digital lending platforms serve diverse borrower pools (salaried professionals, small business owners, first-time credit applicants). The challenge is to maximize profitability while minimizing defaults and maintaining high customer satisfaction by balancing marketing, credit risk assessment, and operational efficiency.

### Core Challenges
- **Identifying underperforming borrower segments** needing revised risk assessment
- **Determining top-performing loan purposes** with highest profitability and lowest defaults
- **Analyzing risk-return tradeoffs** across different credit grades
- **Optimizing portfolio allocation** to reduce risk and improve margins
- **Investigating profitability variance** between high-risk and low-risk borrower segments
